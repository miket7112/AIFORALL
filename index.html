Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines. These machines are programmed to think and perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as problem-solving, pattern recognition, and decision-making. AI is transforming industries like healthcare, finance, entertainment, and more.
